The Murwa Family Heritage

The Murwa family traces its origins to Western Kenya, where our ancestors built a home based on faith, integrity, and unity. Over the years, our family has spread across regions but continues to share the same love and respect.

Each generation has contributed unique talents and achievements — from teachers to farmers, leaders to innovators — all carrying forward the Murwa name with pride.

Our elders always remind us that family is not just blood — it is a bond of shared values and mutual support. This website keeps their memory alive.
