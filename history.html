The Bosslady Family Heritage

The Bosslady family proudly originates from Eldoret Sub County, Turbo, Ngenyilel Ward, Kapkechui area. Our story is one of resilience, faith, and unity — led by our beloved mother Aggary Anjiko, a strong widow who raised her family with grace and love.

We are a family of five siblings — Harriet Camilla, Emily Venrander,Bravin Murwa Cyprian Murwa, and Beled Dilo — bound by shared memories, laughter, and the dream of continuing our family’s legacy.

Through challenges and blessings, we stand together. The Murwa family believes that unity is strength, and our story will live on through generations — built on love, hard work, and the wisdom of those who came before us.
